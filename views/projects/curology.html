Curology

Curology, produces and ships prescription skincare products for acne and anti-aging. The Company offers medications for various treatments that include acne, clogged pores, dark spots, skin firmness, texture, and wrinkles. Curology serves customers in the State of California.

The colors of the brand are mainly two shades, i.e. white and purple, which is noticeable mainly in the colors of the products. What is more, Curology also focuses on minimalis, not stuffing any additional texts on product, except the brand name .In the photo shot, I tried to take into account all these characteristics as well as the original place of the company, i.e. California.
