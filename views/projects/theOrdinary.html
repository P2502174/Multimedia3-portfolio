THE ORDINARY

Despite the name, there is nothing ordinary about skincare industry champion The Ordinary. Delivering quality skin care at an affordable price, this no-nonsense brand has taken the beauty world by storm, with skin gurus and industry masters alike singing its praises. From banishing blemishes and minimising pores to reducing wrinkles and evening out skin tone, there is something for everyone.

The brand focuses primarily on minimalism. Simple glass bottles with aesthetically contrasting labels are a metaphor for 100% natural products made without harmful additional fragrances. Taking into account the style of the brand, set up was also styled in a minimalistic way.
