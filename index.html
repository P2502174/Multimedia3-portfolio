PRE-PRODUCTION

MY PRE-PRODUCTION PROCESS IS MAINLY AN ANALYSIS OF THE BRAND, ITS APPROACH TO THE PRODUCT AND AESTHETICS. THEN I PREPARE THE SESSION PLAN, ALL BACKDROP AND PROPS AS WELL AS COMPOSITIONS. USUALLY, THIS PROCESS TAKES ME UP TO A WEEK

PRODUCTION

PRODUCTION IS THE TIME WHEN THE MAGIC BEGINS. CREATIVITY IS THE MOST IMPORTANT THING IN THIS PROCESS. THE ARRANGEMENT OF THE SET, PRODUCTS AND COMPOSITIONS ARE CHANGED TO GET THE BEST RESULTS. IN ADDITION, IN THE PRODUCTION THERE IS A CHANCE TO TEST DIFFERENT LENSES, EQUIPMENT AND THEIR SETTINGS. THIS IS THE MOST TIME-CONSUMING STAGE, BUT ALSO MY FAVOURITE ONE.

POST PRODUCTION

POST-PRODUCTION IS THE SELECTION AND EDITING OF EFFECTS FROM PRODUCTION. MOST OFTEN I USE THE BASICS OF RETOUCHING IN THIS PROCESS TO GET RID OF SHARP EDGES AND ENHANCE THE COLOR. FOR THIS PURPOSE, I USE PHOTOSHOP AND ILLUSTRATOR.
